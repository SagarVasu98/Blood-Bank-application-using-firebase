DONATION PROCESS

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

1: REGISTRATION

You need to complete a very simple   ,
registration form. Which contains all
required contact information to enter
in the donation process.

2: SCREENING

A drop of blood from your finger will
take for simple test to ensure that
your blood iron levels are proper
enough for donation process.

3: DONATION

After ensuring and passed screening
test successfully you will be directed
It will take only 6-10 minutes.
enter in the donation process.

4: REFRESHMENT

You can also stay in sitting room until
you feel strong enough to leave our center
You will receive awesome
drink from us in donation zone.
